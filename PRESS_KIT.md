## ğŸ“– **Story & Setting**

In ancient Israel, twelve tribes received unique blessings from their father Jacob. Each tribe was given a precious gemstone to be woven into the high priest's breastplate - the Ephod. Players journey through tribal lands, collecting stones, memorizing scripture, and discovering creation science facts.

**"No violence. No stats. No loot. Only heart, unity, and discovery."**

### **Complete Tribal Experience**

- **12 Unique Worlds**: Desert camps, mountain villages, coastal harbors, forest clearings
- **48 Diverse Avatars**: 4 avatars per tribe (ages 12-29) with varied appearances
- **Authentic Scripture**: NIV/KJV verses with tribal context
- **Real Science Facts**: Butterfly wings, eagle vision, olive tree longevity

- **Side Quests**: Collectible butterflies and flowers with nature facts

### **Educational Value**

- **Cultural Appreciation**: Respectful representation of ancient traditions
