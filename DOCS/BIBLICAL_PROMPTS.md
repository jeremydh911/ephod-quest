# Biblical Accuracy Verification Prompts

## Purpose

This document provides structured prompts for AI assistants, biblical scholars, and human experts to verify the biblical accuracy, theological soundness, and cultural respect of content in the *Twelve Stones* game.

---

## General Verification Prompt

```
You are a biblical scholar and theologian with expertise in the Old Testament, 
specifically the twelve tribes of Israel. Please review the following game content 
for accuracy, theological soundness, and cultural respect.

Content Type: [Narrative/Dialogue/Scripture/Historical Fact/Character Description]
Tribe: [Name of Tribe]
Content: [Paste content here]

Please evaluate based on these criteria:

1. BIBLICAL ACCURACY
   - Are the facts historically and scripturally correct?
   - Are scripture references accurate and properly contextualized?
   - Are there any contradictions with biblical accounts?

2. THEOLOGICAL SOUNDNESS
   - Does the content align with orthodox Christian theology?
   - Are there any unintended theological implications?
   - Is the portrayal respectful of different Christian traditions?

3. CULTURAL RESPECT
   - Is the portrayal of ancient Hebrew culture respectful and accurate?
   - Are there any cultural appropriations or misrepresentations?
   - Is the content sensitive to Jewish heritage?

4. CHARACTER REPRESENTATION
   - Are biblical figures portrayed with appropriate reverence?
   - Are their strengths and weaknesses balanced?
   - Is the characterization consistent with biblical accounts?

5. AGE APPROPRIATENESS
   - Is the content suitable for all ages?
   - Are difficult topics (violence, judgment) handled appropriately?
   - Does it maintain educational value while being accessible?

Please provide:
- An overall accuracy score (1-10)
- Specific issues or concerns (if any)
- Suggestions for improvement
- Sources for any corrections
```

## Final Approval Checklist

Before content is finalized, verify:

- [ ] Scripture references checked against original texts
- [ ] Historical facts verified against multiple sources
- [ ] Theological implications reviewed by diverse experts
- [ ] Cultural elements approved by sensitivity consultants
- [ ] Age-appropriate content confirmed by education specialists
- [ ] Legal review for copyright (if using specific translations)
- [ ] Denominational diversity respected (Catholic/Protestant/Orthodox)
- [ ] Jewish perspective consulted and respected
- [ ] Educational value confirmed
- [ ] Entertainment value maintained
