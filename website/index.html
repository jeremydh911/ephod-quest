The Story

Ancient wisdom meets modern adventure

In the ancient lands of Israel, twelve tribes received precious gemstones from their father Jacob. Each stone represented unique blessings and characteristics that would shape their destiny.

These sacred stones were woven into the high priest's breastplate - the Ephod - a symbol of unity and divine guidance. Now, you are called to journey through each tribal land, discover their stories, collect their stones, and weave them together in a grand finale of cooperation and celebration.

"No violence. No stats. No loot. Only heart, unity, and discovery."

About the Developers

Faith-driven game development

Twelve Stones: Ephod Quest was created by Jeremiah and Shawna Harlin, a husband-wife team passionate about combining faith, education, and entertainment.

With backgrounds in software development and education, they set out to create games that make biblical learning engaging and accessible to families and faith communities worldwide.

"We believe games can be powerful tools for spiritual growth and family bonding."
